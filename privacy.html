Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

✅ TL;DR: Your text never leaves your browser. We do not collect, store, or share any data. No registration required. No cookies used for tracking.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. When you use Word Counter Tool, the text you input is processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • No text is sent to our servers
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • No personal information is collected
  • Your text disappears when you close or refresh the page

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, there is nothing to use. Your text remains completely private and under your control at all times.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We only use a minimal local storage setting to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). This is stored locally on your device and is not sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place. Our website may contain links to external sites, but we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Data Security

Since your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your own device's security measures. We implement standard security practices on our servers to protect the website itself, but your text input is never touching our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our website is accessible to users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date below. Since we fundamentally do not collect data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
