About Word Counter Tool

Free online text analysis tools for everyone

Our Mission

Word Counter Tool was created to provide free, fast, and reliable text analysis tools for writers, students, marketers, and anyone who needs to count words, characters, or analyze their text. We believe that everyone should have access to powerful text analysis tools without paying anything or compromising their privacy.

What We Offer

  • Word Counter - Count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in real-time
  • Character Counter - Detailed breakdown of letters, numbers, spaces, and punctuation
  • Sentence Counter - Count sentences and analyze sentence structure
  • Paragraph Counter - Count paragraphs and get writing statistics
  • Keyword Density Analyzer - Analyze keyword frequency and density for SEO optimization

  • Privacy First - Your text never leaves your browser. No data is stored or uploaded.
  • No Registration - Start using our tools immediately without signing up
  • Fast & Reliable - Get instant results with our optimized tools
